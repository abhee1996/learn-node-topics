Fee Payment Advice

LYIT offers various methods for fee payment as follows: 

  • Online Payments – students may pay online from here. You'll need your username and password.
  • Bank Transfer can be made directly to the LYIT Student Fees bank account.  Bank details available on request from admissions@lyit.ie 
  • Bank Drafts – drafts can be made payable to Letterkenny Institute of Technology (LYIT)
  • Cheques – cheques can be made payable to Letterkenny Institute of Technology (LYIT)
  • International Student Payments – international student payments should be made via Flywire.

Please note that the Admissions Office does not accept cash payment for fees. Receipts are available on the student portal. 

Payment Schedule

Payment terms are set out as follows and student fees are required to be paid by these dates:

Cat Student Type What is due? Payment Deadline
A Full-time Undergraduate qualifying for the Free Fees Scheme Student Contribution Charge (€3,000 for 2021/22) Note that students who are awarded a full grant may have this charge paid by the relevant grant authority Payment 1: 25% of Student Contribution & Tuition Fee Prior to registration
Payment 2: 25% of Student Contribution & Tuition Fee Prior by 31 October
Payment 3: Remaining 50% before end of January
D Part-time Student on Programmes of at least 60 Credits Programme Fee Payment 1  50% prior to Registration Payment 2:  remaining 50% before end of January
E Part-time Student on Programmes of less than 60 Credits Programme Fee 100% of fee due prior to Registration
F Non-EU Non-EU Programme Fee 100% of fee due prior to Registration

Monthly Instalments

Students in categories A, B, C and D may alternatively apply to pay their fees in monthly instalments as follows:

  • 25% of full fee prior to registration, with the remainder payable in monthly instalments before 7 June.

Students who wish to pay their fees by monthly instalment will be registered upon receipt of the completed agreement form and payment of the first Instalment.  Monthly instalments should be paid by logging into your student account.  You may also query your current outstanding balance from this link.  Note, by completing the instalment agreement form you are agreeing to pay by monthly instalment; therefore, failure to do so will result in late fee penalties.

Instalment Agreement Form

Note

LYIT reserves the right to change a student’s fee status during the academic year, where additional or new information is presented, or where an error in the calculation of their fees has been discovered.
